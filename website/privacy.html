Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

GroceryLister helps you manage recipes and grocery lists. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Operator: GroceryLister is operated by .
Email: support@grocerylisterapp.com

Information we collect

  • Account information — When you sign in with Google, we receive basic profile information from your Google account, such as your name, email address, and user ID.
  • Content you create — Recipes, grocery lists, ingredients, check-off status, favorites, and other data you save in the app.
  • Device and local storage — When you are signed in, the app may store a copy of your recipes and lists in your browser or on your device so the app works offline and can sync when you reconnect.
  • Technical information — Our hosting and database providers may process standard service logs and security data needed to operate the app reliably.

We do not use dedicated third-party analytics or advertising trackers in the app today.

How we use information

  • Provide, sync, and back up your recipes and grocery lists across devices
  • Enable shared recipe and grocery list links that you choose to send
  • Queue and apply changes made while you are offline
  • Maintain, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the service
  • Respond to support requests and account deletion requests

How sharing works

We do not sell your personal information.

Recipe links — If you share a recipe link, anyone with that URL can view the recipe. Recipe links are not time-limited.

Important: Recipes in GroceryLister can be viewed by anyone who has the recipe link or recipe ID. Links are not protected by a password, and they do not expire automatically. Only share recipe links with people you trust, and avoid posting them publicly if you want to keep a recipe private.

Grocery list links — If a list owner shares a list for shopping, anyone with the link can view the list and check items off while the share window is active (currently 7 days, reset when the owner shares again). Guests cannot change list structure.

You are responsible for who you send links to. Only share links with people you trust.

Service providers

GroceryLister uses Google Firebase and Google Cloud services for authentication, hosting, and database storage. Google processes data on our behalf according to its own policies. Learn more at Firebase Privacy and Security and Google Privacy Policy.

Data retention

We keep your account data while your account is active so the service can function. If you request account deletion, we delete or de-identify associated account data within a reasonable period, except where we must retain information for legal, security, or backup reasons.

Your choices

  • You can stop using the service and sign out at any time.
  • You control whether and with whom you share recipe or grocery list links.
  • You may delete your account in the app under Account → Delete account, or request deletion by emailing support@grocerylisterapp.com.

Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal information. We will honor valid requests to the extent required by applicable law.

All users

  • Request access to or correction of personal information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Stop using the service and sign out at any time

European Economic Area, UK, and Switzerland

If GDPR or similar laws apply to you, you may also have the right to restrict or object to certain processing, request data portability, and lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. Our legal basis for processing is generally performing our contract with you (providing the service), your consent where required (such as sign-in), and our legitimate interests in operating and securing GroceryLister.

California and certain U.S. states

We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. California residents may request disclosure of categories of personal information collected and request deletion, subject to legal exceptions. To exercise these rights, email support@grocerylisterapp.com.

We will verify requests where appropriate and respond within the timeframes required by applicable law.

Children

GroceryLister is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the date at the top of this page. Continued use of GroceryLister after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy: support@grocerylisterapp.com
